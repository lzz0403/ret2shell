## Welcome to the Game!

We hope you'll enjoy a high-quality game here, hacking only for knowledge and fun. To enhance your experience, we kindly ask you to adhere to the following guidelines:

1. Please follow the game rules and refrain from using any cheating methods. Avoid discussing game-related information or solutions with anyone other than your teammates, and definitely do not share flags or writeups.
2. Abide by community norms and avoid posting any unfriendly remarks in the game IM group.
3. Until the game archives, refrain from discussing problem details or strategies in public.
4. After the game ends and until the game is archived, do not publicly share your complete solutions.
5. The organizers may have additional instructions, which will be declared before the game. All rights reserved to the organizers.

Violating these guidelines may result in disqualification or account suspension. We believe that most players are friendly, and we hope you are too! ΦωΦ

## Additional Considerations

In addition to the above guidelines, we encourage you to:

1. When asking questions, refer to ["How To Ask Questions The Smart Way"](http://www.catb.org/~esr/faqs/smart-questions.html) to help us assist you better. Also, remember to protect your personal privacy when posting questions.
2. In beginner-oriented games, refrain from making verbal attacks or trolling based on skill level. Let's maintain a friendly environment.
3. In more serious formal games, avoid unnecessary interactions with other players.

## Best of luck!

We wish you a smooth game experience and great results! 🌟
